Contact

Drop me a line...

Email is by far the best way to get in touch with me. I try to respond within 12 hours to every single email I get. Feel free to ask me a question, even if you're in the very early stages of your project and don't really know exactly what you need. I can help you get to the next step.

Set up a meeting...

Often times, a project will require more direct communication than emails alone can provide. A face-to-face meeting is a great way to kick off a project to ensure that everyone is on the same page. If you're in the Austin, TX area, I am avaible to meet in person during normal business hours. Otherwise, I can do video conference calls on Skype or Google Hangouts. Email me to set up a time.
